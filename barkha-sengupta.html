Table of Biography

Barkha Sengupta is a popular Indian Television and film actress. Barkha Sengupta is famous for her role as Anjani in the Indian TV series, “Sankatmochan Mahabali Hanuman”.

Early Life and Childhood

Barkha Sengupta was born on 28th December 1979 in Hisar, Haryana, India. She is also known as Barkha Bisht.

Barkha is currently 42 years old and she holds Indian citizenship. Likewise, she follows Hinduism and her birth sign is Capricorn.

Moving on to her family members, she hasn’t revealed the details regarding her parents. However, she spent her early childhood in military towns in India especially in Kolkata, as her father was a colonel.

Likewise, she comes from a Garhwali family and belongs to Dehradun in Uttarakhand. Moreover, she is the youngest child of her parents and has two sisters.

Her eldest sister, Arpana Bisht is a hotel management professional, and her elder sister, Sapna Bisht is a fashion designer. She loved dancing from an early age.

Education

In terms of her educational status, she did her schooling at Kendriya Vidyalaya Fortwilliam in Kolkata. After schooling, she joined Symbiosis College in Pune, India, and completed her graduation there.

Relationship Status

Regarding her personal life, Barkha Sengupta is currently in a married relationship with her husband, Indraneil Sengupta. Indraneil is also an Indian film and television actor.

The couple started dating each other in 2006 and finally married each other on 2nd March 2008. The marriage took place in Haridwar.

Likewise, the couple became parents of their first child; a daughter named Meera Sengupta in October 2011.

There are rumors of their breaking up since they seem to stay separately. However, they have not spoken about it.

Barkha Sengupta – Net Worth 2023

Being a celebrated Indian actress, she has made a pretty handful amount of money. As of 2022, Barkha has an estimated net worth of $1-$5 million US dollars. She made this wealth from her acting career.

Career and Professional Life

Barkha began her television career with an Indian TV series, “Kitni Mast Hai Zindagi”, which aired on MTV India. She appeared in it from 2004 to 2005.

Likewise, in 2005, she appeared in three TV series titled, “Kasautii Zindagii Kay”, “Kkavyanjali”, and “Kaisa Ye Pyar Hai”.

Similarly, she then starred in “Pyaar Ke Do Naam: Ek Raadha, Ek Shyaam” on Star Plus.

Here, she portrayed the key characters of Shyaama, Raadha, and Radhika. She also presented a film-based program named “Popcorn” on Zoom TV.

Moreover, she performed the lead role of Anupama in the Sahara One channel’s “Doli Saja Ke. Kasautii Zindagii Kay”.

Also, “Kyaa Hoga Nimmo Kaa”, and “Kkavyanjali” all featured her in cameo roles. Moving on, she also took part in the reality show “Saas vs. Bahu”.

Likewise, she also played the lead role of Dhani in the TV series “Sajan Ghar Jaana Hai”.

She went on to play Hanuman’s mother Ajan in the Sony TV drama “Sankatmochan Mahabali Hanuman”, as well as the starring role of Prema Shalini in SAB TV’s “Shrimaan Shrimati Phir Se”.

Similarly, she also appeared in Kaal Bhairav Rahasya Season 2 as Bhairavi.

Also, she appeared in the “Chandragupta Maurya” and “Paramavatar Shri Krishna” serials as Tarini, Dhana Nanda’s wife, and Draupadi, respectively. She also appeared in Star Plus’ “Shaadi Mubarak” as Nandini.

Apart from these, she also has appeared in many movies. Some of them include “Rajneeti” (2010), ” Ami Shubhash Bolchi” (2011), “Villain” (2013), and many more.
