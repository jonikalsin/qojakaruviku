In his online bio he says: ‘Having grown up in the entertainment industry I have been obsessed with how to use a lens to tell stories from a very young age.

He added: ‘I learned on film and I feel this has truly shaped me as a photographer and has given me a deeper understanding of light and composition.

‘My goal for each of my clients is to create a unique photo-experience, while capturing beautiful memories and creative images. Plus I’m a bit of a goof, so we will have fun while we do it.’
