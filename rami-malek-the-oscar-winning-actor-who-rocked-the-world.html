Rami Malek: The Oscar-Winning Actor Who Rocked the World

Rami Malek is one of the most talented and versatile actors in Hollywood today.

He has won numerous awards and accolades for his roles in film and television.

Including the Academy Award for Best Actor for his portrayal of Freddie Mercury in the biopic Bohemian Rhapsody (2018).

He is also known for his captivating performance as Elliot Alderson in the critically acclaimed series Mr. Robot (2015-2019).

In this article, we will tell you more about Rami Malek, his background, his career, and his personal life.

Who is Rami Malek?

Rami Malek was born on May 12, 1981, in Torrance, California, to Egyptian immigrant parents.

His father was a tour guide in Cairo who later sold insurance, and his mother was an accountant.

He has an identical twin brother named Sami, who is a teacher, and an older sister named Yasmine, who is a medical doctor.

He was raised in the Coptic Orthodox faith and attended Notre Dame High School in Sherman Oaks, California, where he graduated in 1999.

Moreover, he developed an interest in acting at a young age and participated in several school plays and musicals.

He also took theater classes at the University of Evansville in Indiana, where he earned his Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in 2003.

Then, he moved to New York City to pursue his acting career and landed his first TV role in an episode of Gilmore Girls in 2004.

How did Rami Malek become famous?

Rami Malek’s breakthrough came in 2006, when he was cast as Pharaoh Ahkmenrah in the comedy film Night at the Museum, alongside Ben Stiller and Robin Williams.

He reprised his role in the sequels Night at the Museum: Battle of the Smithsonian (2009) and Night at the Museum: Secret of the Tomb (2014).

He also appeared in various TV shows and films, such as The War at Home (2005-2007), The Pacific (2010), Larry Crowne (2011).

The Master (2012), The Twilight Saga: Breaking Dawn – Part 2 (2012), Ain’t Them Bodies Saints (2013), and Short Term 12 (2013)².

However, his career reached new heights in 2015, when he was chosen to play the lead role of Elliot Alderson, a hacker and vigilante, in the USA Network series Mr. Robot, created by Sam Esmail. .

In 2018, Malek achieved his biggest success to date, when he starred as Freddie Mercury, the legendary frontman of the rock band Queen, in the biopic Bohemian Rhapsody, directed by Bryan Singer.

The film was a huge box office hit and a cultural phenomenon, and Malek received universal praise for his performance.

He won the Academy Award for Best Actor, becoming the first actor of Egyptian heritage to do so, as well as the Golden Globe Award, the Screen Actors Guild Award, the BAFTA Award, and many more.

What is Rami Malek doing now?

Rami Malek is currently working on several projects, both in film and television.

He is also producing and starring in a podcast series called Blackout.

This is a thriller about a small-town radio DJ who must fight to protect his family and community from a nationwide power outage.

Also, he is involved in a number of humanitarian and philanthropic causes.

Such as raising awareness and funds for refugees, supporting LGBTQ+ rights, and promoting diversity and inclusion in the entertainment industry.

Personal Life

Rami Malek is not married, but he is in a relationship with his Bohemian Rhapsody co-star Lucy Boynton.

Who played Freddie Mercury’s love interest, Mary Austin, in the film.

The couple confirmed their romance in January 2019.

When Malek thanked Boynton in his acceptance speech at the Palm Springs International Film Festival.

They have since appeared together at various events and award shows and have expressed their love and admiration for each other.

Conclusion

Rami Malek is an Oscar-winning actor who rocked the world with his portrayal of Freddie Mercury in Bohemian Rhapsody.

He is also known for his role as Elliot Alderson in Mr. Robot and as Pharaoh Ahkmenrah in Night at the Museum.

He is a talented and versatile actor who can play diverse and complex characters.

Also, he is a passionate and generous person who supports various causes and initiatives.

Indeed, he is one of the most influential and inspiring actors in Hollywood today.
