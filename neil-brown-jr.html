Neil Brown Jr. Biography

Neil Brown Jr. is best known as Actor, martial artist, singer who has an estimated Net Worth of $3 Million. American actor who played a role in the martial arts television series WMAC Masters and portrayed Guillermo on the television series The Walking Dead. He also appeared as DJ Yella in the 2015 film Straight Outta Compton. In 2019, he began appearing in CBS's SEAL Team. He was born on June 19,1980, Florida. He guest-starred on the sketch comedy show King Bachelor's Pad. He played one of his first roles as Jake in WMAC Masters in 1995. He co-starred with Colin Farrell in the 2000 film Tigerland. He is of mixed African American descent and grew up with family in Orlando, Florida. In 2000 he married Catrina Robinson Brown and they welcomed two children. His zodiac sign is Gemini.
