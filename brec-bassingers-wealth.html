About Brec Bassinger

Born on May 25, 1999, in Saginaw, Texas, Brec Bassinger is an actress known for her portrayal of Bella Dawson in the TV series “Bella and the Bulldogs”. She has also appeared as Emma Hawking in “The Haunted Hathaways” and has taken on various other roles throughout her career.

As of early 2018, Brec Bassinger is estimated to have a net worth of $200,000, earned through her successful career in the entertainment industry, which began in 2013.

Brec Bassinger’s Earnings

Coming from a challenging childhood due to her father’s early departure and dealing with diabetes type 1, Brec began working at a young age. She started acting at 14, with her debut role as Emma Hawking in “The Haunted Hathaways” from 2013 to 2014, which significantly contributed to her net worth. In 2015, she gained recognition for her role as Bella Dawson in the TV series “Bella and the Bulldogs”, and also starred in the television film “Liar, Liar, Vampire”. Furthermore, Brec has appeared in “Forever Boys” and “School of Rock” and has featured in several other productions, all of which have added to her net worth.

Currently, she is involved in the comedy film “Saturday at the Starlight”, set for release in late 2018.

In her personal life, Brec has been in a relationship with actor Dylan Summerall since 2017, and has been actively involved in raising awareness regarding type 1 diabetes.
