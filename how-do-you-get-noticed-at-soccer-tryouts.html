How do you get noticed at soccer tryouts?

July 2024 Â· 4 minute read

Soccer tryouts can be a nerve-wracking experience for any soccer player, especially if you're hoping to get noticed by a college coach or a professional team scout. But with the right preparation and mindset, you can increase your chances of standing out and impressing the decision-makers at the tryout.

One of the most effective ways to get noticed at soccer tryouts is by creating a standout recruiting video. A recruiting video is a short highlight reel that showcases your skills, athleticism, and soccer IQ. Make sure to include footage of your best plays and moments, as well as your versatility and ability to play multiple positions. But keep in mind that your recruiting video should also showcase your personality and character, as coaches are not just looking for talented players, but also for team players who will fit in with their program.

Another important step in getting noticed at soccer tryouts is by reaching out to college coaches and professional teams. Start by researching schools and programs that match your soccer goals and academic interests. Look for programs that have a history of recruiting players like yourself, as well as coaches who have a track record of developing players and helping them succeed on and off the field. Once you have a list of potential programs, reach out to the coaches and introduce yourself. Let them know about your interest in their program, your soccer background, and your academic achievements. Be polite, respectful, and professional in your email, and don't forget to attach your recruiting video.

It is also important to stay organized and focused throughout the recruitment process. Keep track of the schools and programs you have reached out to, as well as their response and feedback. This will help you stay on top of your recruiting efforts and avoid missing out on any opportunities. It is important to remember that the recruitment process can be long and challenging, but the key is to stay patient and persistent.

If you're looking to get noticed at soccer tryouts and secure a D1 offer, there are specific steps you can take to increase your chances of success. Crafting compelling emails is an important part of reaching out to college coaches, and can make a big difference in how they perceive you as a player and a person. Make sure to customize your emails to each coach, and highlight why you're interested in their program and how you can contribute to their team. Be specific and detailed in your email, and don't forget to attach your recruiting video.

Attending showcases and camps is another effective way to get noticed by college coaches and professional team scouts. Look for showcases and camps that match your soccer goals and interests, and that attract coaches from programs you're interested in. This will give you the opportunity to showcase your skills and potential in front of decision-makers, as well as to learn from experienced coaches and players. Make sure to come prepared and ready to give your best effort, and to showcase your skills and versatility in different positions and situations.
