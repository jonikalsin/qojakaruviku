Julianne Moore popularly known as Julie Anne Smith is an American actress, author and producer. As of 2023, Julianne Moore’s net worth is $55 million. She accumulated her net worth through acting in movies, writing books, and producing movies, shows, and theatricals. She is known for her emotionally compelling roles.

While Moore started acting in school, her professional acting career didn’t begin before she graduated. Moore started her acting career in theater. After graduating with a degree in Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) in 1983, she moved to New York at the age of 22 to pursue a career in acting. She had to be a waitress for a few months before finding work in a regional theater. 

Her earliest acting gigs were those in the theater, where she brought the learnings from her Fine Arts degree to practice. She had to adopt a new stage name as there were already actors at the time named Julie Anne Smith and Julie Smith, which explains why she chose the name Julianne Moore (Anne from her mother and Moore from her father).
