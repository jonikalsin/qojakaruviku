Lil Wayne's Kids - Meet The Rapper's 4 Children With 4 women

August 2024 · 4 minute read

The famous American rapper, singer, and record executive, Lil Wayne, has four kids with four different women. His children are Reginae Carter, 25, Dwayne Carter III, 15, Kameron Carter, 14, and Neal Carter, 14. He shares his kids with his ex-wife Toya Johnson, Sarah Vivian, Lauren London, and Nivea, in that order. 

Despite having what appears to be a complex family life, the Grammy-award-winning entertainer has said he appreciates being active in his children’s lives against all odds.

Meet all Lil Wayne’s Children and Their Mothers

1. Reginae Carter

Reginae Djuan Carter's Profile

  • Full name: Reginae Djuan Carter
  • Date of Birth: November 29, 1998
  • Place of Birth: New Orleans, Louisiana, USA
  • Age:  25 years old
  • Zodiac Sign: Sagittarius
  • Mother: Toya Johnson
  • Career: Actress, author, and Model

Reginae is Wayn’s oldest and only daughter who was born on November 29, 1998, when her father was only 16. She is a product of his relationship with his high school sweetheart Toya Johnson. Her parents are known to have married on Valentine’s Day of 2004 and divorced in 2006.

Meanwhile, Reginae is currently reckoned as an American actress, singer, author and TV personality. She is popularly known for her work in movies like Boxed In (2022), Terror Lake Drive (2020) and I Got a Story to Tell (2022).

She also co-authored the book Paparazzi Princesses and was formerly a member of the girl group OMG Girlz.

In addition, Reginae amassed a sizable online fan base both during and after the coronavirus outbreak. With a line of athletic wear, Lil Wayne’s daughter is a businesswoman in addition to being an entertainer.

The 25 years old celebrity daughter is currently dating Armon Warren (Armoney).

2. Dwayne Carter III

Dwayne's Profile

  • Full name: Dwayne Carter III
  • Date of Birth: October 22, 2008
  • Place of Birth: Cincinnati, Ohio, USA

Dwayne was born on the 22nd of October 2008  at The Christ Hospital in Cincinnati Ohio to Lil Wayne and the radio broadcaster Sarah Vivan. The relationship between the rapper and Sarah lasted from 2005 until 2008 when they went their separate ways. They were already engaged in July 2007 before they called it off the next year.

Kameron Carter was birthed by Lil Wayne and actress and model, Lauren London who later became even more popular as the fiancee of the late American rapper, Nipsey Hussle.

According to London, she first met Wayne when she was 15 years old and began a relationship that could not last. She claims that despite numerous attempts to make it work, they ultimately broke up in 2009, the same year Kameron was born.

In the meantime, Kameron has inherited his father’s musical talent and rapped in Lil Wayne’s 2020 track titled “Kam.”

4. Neal Carter

Neal's Profile

  • Full name: Neal Carter
  • Date of Birth: November 30, 2009
  • Place of Birth: Birmingham, Alabama
  • Age: 14 years old
  • Zodiac Sign: Sagittarius
  • Mother: Nivea

The fourth and final child of well-known rapper Dwayne Carter Jr., commonly known as Lil Wayne, is Neal Carter. Wayne’s romance with Grammy-nominated singer, Nivea resulted in the birth of the child. Nivea and Lil Wayne began dating in February 2002 and got engaged in December of the same year.

Nivea previously claimed that her first love was Wayne and declared that she would pass away a single woman if they did not get married. However, the estranged couple broke up in August 2003. Nivea later wed R&B musician and producer Terius “The-Dream” Nash in December 2004, but their union ultimately ended in divorce in 2007. Nivea and Lil Wayne reunited following her divorce in 2007.

There were rumors that Nivea and Lil Wayne were engaged and expecting a child on June 9, 2009. Meanwhile, Lil Wayne and actress Lauren London were also expecting a child at the same time. To clear the air, Lil Wayne announced that he and Nivea were expecting a boy on October 14, 2009. On November 30, 2009, Nivea gave birth to their baby, Neal Carter. Nivea and Lil Wayne ended their engagement a second time in June 2010.

Just like his father, the kid has already been showing glimpses of his rap prowess. He has notably been featured in a number of his father’s music videos and with commitment and passion he may be carving a niche for himself in the entertainment industry sooner than later.
