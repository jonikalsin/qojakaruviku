What venue is the 2024 NFL Draft?

May 2024 · 3 minute read

The 2024 NFL Draft is set to take place in Detroit, Michigan, and promises to be an exhilarating three-day event for football enthusiasts. Let's delve into the details of this grand event.

Location and Date

The 2024 NFL Draft will be held in Detroit from April 25 to 27.

The primary venues for the Draft Experience Presented by Rocket Mortgage are Hart Plaza and Corner Ballpark.

The Draft Experience will be spread across these locations, offering a myriad of activities and attractions for fans.

Draft Experience Presented by Rocket Mortgage

The NFL Draft Experience Presented by Rocket Mortgage is a free fan festival that invites both Detroit residents and visitors to immerse themselves in the world of football.

The event will feature immersive exhibits, sponsor activations, and the opportunity to take photos with the prestigious Vince Lombardi Trophy.

Also, fans can enjoy the youth-focused Play 60 Zone, shop for exclusive merchandise at the NFL Shop presented by Visa, and even score autographs from current NFL players and Legends at the Autograph Stage.

Hours of Operation

The Draft Experience will be open to the public as follows:

  • Thursday, April 25: 12:00 p.m. – 10:00 p.m.
  • Friday, April 26: 12:00 p.m. – 10:00 p.m.
  • Saturday, April 27: 9:00 a.m. – 6:00 p.m.

Activities and Offerings

The Draft Experience will not only showcase the excitement of the NFL Draft but also highlight Detroit's unique culture in music, art, food, and style. Here are some of the key offerings:

  • Food and beverage vendors showcasing local fare, including soul food, Greek, Mexican, Middle Eastern, and East African cuisines.
  • A celebration of the Detroit Hustle through dance, with fan participation encouraged across Campus Martius Park on Day 3 of the Draft.
  • Youth-focused activities at Corner Ballpark, including NFL FLAG skills and drills and football clinics.

Draft Day in the D

In addition to the main Draft Experience, Draft Day in the D is a three-day celebration across downtown Detroit's parks.

The festivities will be located at Capitol Park, Beacon Park, Paradise Valley Beatrice Buck Park, Grand Circus Park, and a closed-off portion of Woodward Avenue between State and Witherell streets.

This family-friendly event will feature large screens showing live NFL Draft action, Bud Light beer tents, live entertainment, and much more. Notable offerings include:

  • Showcasing of products by 45 local vendors, including All Things Detroit Marketplace, Detroit Originals, Poppa's Gourmet Hot Sauce, Frick'n Good Cookies, and Sweet Potato Sensations.
  • More than 60 local Detroit artists, musicians, and entertainers performing.
  • Interactive art installations, such as a custom-made Draft Day in the D football arch and other fan photo opportunities.
  • Daily cornhole tournaments and cigar rolling courtesy of La Casa Cigar Lounge at Paradise Valley Beatrice Buck Park.

Entry and Registration

Entry to the NFL Draft Experience Presented by Rocket Mortgage is free for all attendees.

To gain entry, fans are required to download the NFL OnePass app or register via the website NFL.com/DraftAccess.

All adults are required to register, and each adult can register up to five children to accompany them to the event.

Registration on-site via Fan Services staff is also permitted for fans without a smartphone.

Additional Information

For detailed road closures, event updates, and specific questions, attendees can visit nfl.com/draft/event-info, downtowndetroit.org, and visitdetroit.com.

For parking, attendees can download the ParkWhiz app to access up-to-date parking availability and pricing.

The ParkDetroit app enables attendees to pay for street parking on demand with their phone.

The 2024 NFL Draft promises to be an unforgettable experience for football fans, offering a perfect blend of sports, entertainment, and the vibrant culture of Detroit.

Whether you're a die-hard football enthusiast or simply looking to enjoy a fun-filled weekend, the Draft Experience and Draft Day in the D are set to deliver an extraordinary celebration of the sport.
