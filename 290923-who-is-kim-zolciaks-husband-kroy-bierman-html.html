Who Is Kim Zolciaks Husband Kroy Biermann?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

"Real Housewives of Atlanta" star Kim Zolciak turned 44 on May 19, but she could only share online how she and her famous husband celebrated it one week later.

TV personality Kim Zolciak keeps proving that age is just a number. She celebrated her 44th birthday going to a fancy dinner date and rocking a long-sleeved, skintight royal blue mini dress and matching high heels.

Her famous husband was by her side, and he opted for dark blue pants, a light blue shirt, and black shoes. Scroll down to see the photo and learn more about Kim Zolciak's husband.

Kim Zolciak-Biermann in "Watch What Happens Live" in December 2017 | Source: Getty Images

WHO IS KIM ZOLCIAK MARRIED TO?

Zolciak's spouse, Kroy Biermann, might look familiar to Atlanta Falcons fans as he played as a linebacker for the NFL team from 2008 to 2015.

Biermann and Zolciak met at the Dancing Stars of Atlanta charity event in 2010. The TV personality was so attracted to the athlete that she told him she had never seen a backside like his.

Kim Zolciak-Biermann on November 2, 2017 in Atlanta, Georgia | Source: Getty Images

The couple hit it off right away and walked down the aisle in November 2011. Bravo gave Biermann and Zolciak a reality TV show, "Don't Be Tardy for the Wedding," to capture every ceremony detail.

Luckily, it showed better results than expected. Bravo changed its name to "Don't Be Tardy," and it ran for eight seasons. The reality series followed the couple and how they juggled their lives and careers while enlarging their families.

Bravo canceled the show in May 2021, but their decision didn't affect Zolciak much because she was reportedly ready to move on.

DOES KIM ZOLCIAK HAVE CHILDREN?

Zolciak and Biermann share six children. Their elder kids are Brielle and Ariana, Zolciak's daughters from previous relationships who Biermann legally adopted in 2013.

In February 2022, Brielle and Ariana made headlines after body shamers attacked them online and claimed they underwent plastic surgery. Zolciak defended her daughters and pointed out that they had only cosmetic procedures done to their lips.

She explained that Brielle lost weight because she got a major jaw surgery and couldn't chew food for months. In Ariana's case, she worked hard to get fit and healthy. Zolciak remarked that nobody had liposuction.

Apart from Brielle and Ariana, Zolciak and Biermann share four kids. Their eldest son is Kroy Jagger, also known as KJ. He was born in May 2011, a few months before his parents tied the knot.

Kash joined the family in August 2012, followed by twins Kaia and Kane, born in November 2013. In 2017, Zolciak confessed she tried to convince Biermann to have another kid, but he was not on board, and it never happened.

HOW MUCH DOES KIM ZOLCIAK MAKE PER EPISODE OF "RHOA"?

Before "Don't Be Tardy" – a "Real Housewives of Atlanta" spin-off – went off the air, Zolciak made plenty of money. Sources revealed she pocketed about $80,000 per episode in the final season.

Since season eight had 13 episodes, Zolciak took home approximately $1 million in 2020. Apart from that, Zolciak is also a businesswoman and has a swimwear brand and a skincare line.

Biermann also made millions as an NFL player, and Brielle and Ariana use their social media platforms to promote their mom's collections and subscription boxes. What a successful family!
